We Save Animals

At We Save Animals (WSA) Welfare Trust, compassion and care come first. Founded by Rider Praveen Kumar, an ex-gym instructor who chose a path of selflessness, our trust is dedicated to rescuing and rehabilitating animals in need. From abandoned pets to injured strays and those suffering from old age, blindness, or terminal illnesses, we provide them with a sanctuary where they can live with dignity, love, and comfort. Our mission is to ensure that no animal is left unloved or uncared for.

A Safe Haven for Every Animal

Our shelter is more than just a refuge—it's a second chance at life. We specialize in catering to animals facing challenges such as paralysis, accidents, or abuse, offering them specialized medical care and emotional healing. With a team of passionate volunteers and caregivers, we ensure each animal receives personalized attention and a forever home in our shelter. WSA Welfare Trust is a testament to the belief that kindness can transform lives, not only for animals but for the people who get to witness their recovery and joy.

Join Our Mission

Every effort at WSA is made possible through the support of individuals who share our vision of a world where every animal matters. By volunteering, donating, or adopting, you can be a part of our journey to create a more compassionate society. Together, let’s continue to rescue, rehabilitate , and provide hope to those who cannot speak for themselves. Welcome to a community that celebrates life in all its forms, one rescued animal at a time.
