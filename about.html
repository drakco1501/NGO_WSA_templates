Abount The Founder

Praveen Kumar L, also known as Rider Praveen, is a remarkable individual whose compassion knows no bounds. Once a dedicated gym instructor, he made the selfless decision to leave behind his career to pursue a calling far greater than himself: the welfare and rescue of animals in need. This noble pursuit led him to establish the We Save Animals (WSA) Welfare Trust, a sanctuary of hope for countless animals who have been abandoned, injured, or otherwise left to fend for themselves.

Praveen's shelter serves as a haven for animals facing some of life's toughest challenges. His care extends to a diverse group of residents, including paralysed animals, those who are blind, elderly creatures in their twilight years, and victims of accidents or cruelty. He also embraces terminally ill animals, ensuring their remaining days are filled with love, dignity, and comfort. For each of these beings, the shelter is not just a place of refuge; it is a place where they can find happiness, nutritious food, and a sense of belonging.

Through his unwavering commitment, Praveen has transformed what could have been a life of despair for these animals into a life of hope and joy.
